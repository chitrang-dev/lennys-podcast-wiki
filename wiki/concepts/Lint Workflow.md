# Lint Workflow

## Summary

Linting keeps the wiki coherent as it grows.

## Checks

- Orphan pages
- Duplicate concepts
- Missing cross-links
- Stale claims
- Unresolved contradictions
- Concepts that are repeatedly mentioned but still lack dedicated pages

## Typical Prompt

`Lint this wiki for duplicates, stale claims, weak summaries, and missing cross-links.`
