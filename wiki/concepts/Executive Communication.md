# Executive Communication

## Summary

Executive communication is communication optimized for fast buy-in, clear decisions, and efficient action across written and verbal contexts.

## Key Points

- Sell first, then explain logistics.
- Start by framing why the conversation matters and what input or decision is needed.
- Concision is driven by clarity of thought, not minimal word count.
- Preparation improves brevity, relevance, and confidence.
- Anticipating the most obvious objection reduces avoidable friction.
