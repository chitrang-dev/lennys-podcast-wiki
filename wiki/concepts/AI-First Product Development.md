# AI-First Product Development

## Summary

An AI-first approach starts from the product objective and redesigns the solution around what AI makes possible, rather than treating AI as an add-on.

## Key Points

- Start from the objective, not the existing implementation.
- Reintegrate AI teams with product teams so objectives and incentives are shared.
- Evaluate AI systems on downstream outcomes, not only proximal metrics like clicks.
- Turnarounds may require isolated cohorts or experiments to avoid organizational antibodies.
