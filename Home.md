## Workflow

1. Add transcripts to `raw/inbox/<guest-folder>/transcript.md`
2. Ask Codex to ingest them or run the bulk-ingest workflow
3. Review generated source notes and guest pages in Obsidian
4. Ask cross-episode questions and save durable syntheses into the wiki
5. Periodically lint for duplicate guest names, missing links, and stronger concept pages
